CLD WORKSHOP

In today’s fast-paced world, electronics is the magic mantra to handle most of our daily tasks efficiently and easily. Be it basic appliances like mobile phones, refrigerator, cars, or high tech equipment like CCTV security cameras; everything needs an electronic circuit to function. As a means to enlighten the students to the vast possibilities achievable through knowledge of electronics, the IETE Students’ Chapter, BIT Mesra conducts the Circuit Logic Design(CLD) workshop over a course of three days.

PLC WORKSHOP

A programmable logic controller, PLC, or programmable controller is a digital computer used for automation of typically industrial electromechanical processes, such as control of machinery on factory assembly lines, amusement rides, or light fixtures.Plc is slowly proving to be the mainstay of the industrial automation world. To help students understand this evolving system, the IETE BIT Mesra, Students’ Chapter, organises a 3-Day Programmable Logic Controller workshop.

ML WORKSHOP

Machine learning, being one of the hottest skills in demand today, has all sorts of meanings attached to it, especially after Hollywood's movie studios have gotten into the picture. Films like 'Ex Machina' have tantalized the imaginations of moviegoers the world over and made ML into all sorts of things it really isn't. But living in the real world, IETE takes up the initiative to expose you to the incredible array of tasks and feats you can really perform using this technology (that have nothing to do with androids that can pass the Turing Test) in a two to three days long workshop.

INDUSTRIAL VISIT

In the ordinary business of life, an industry can do anything that a genius can and many other things that it cannot. If you have a genius, the industry will improve it; if you have none, the industry will supply it. Archetypal of its event calendar, the Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) BIT Mesra Students’ Chapter organizes its annual Industrial Visit to various Industries situated in the city or nearby it.The purpose of this initiative is to provide an exposure to the types of machinery and demonstrate their workingn. The trip provides an insight on how to apply theoretical knowledge to practical use.
